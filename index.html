About

Founded in 2020 by Brian Minson, Styx Consulting Inc. was created to better serve companies and people around the world in ensure the implementation of technology in the workplace. The goal is to improve the systems and processes used everyday by employees through technology so they can become simplier and more efficient, which means saving time and money.

Technology is changing rapidly, more so than the last 30 years. It can be a struggle to keep up and understand what to invest in to maximize your productivity with your investment. This is where we step in, to help guide you in the right direction to best suit your needs as a business to meet the demands of the market and stay competitive with ample oppurtunities for growth.

Through our world class engineers, marketing team, and financial advisors, we can develop a strategy that can tackle the growing needs of your business, from network infrastructure, training and certifications, marketing, IT support, etc. We can provide it all and more as we are constantly learning about the latest technology being developed and how it can best suit our clients.
